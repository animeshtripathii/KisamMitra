ABOUT US

Our Mission

KisanMitra was founded with a simple goal: to make modern agricultural knowledge and technology accessible to every Indian farmer, regardless of farm size, education level, or digital literacy.

We believe that technology, when properly adapted to local needs, can dramatically improve farming outcomes and rural livelihoods across India.

Our team combines expertise in agriculture, technology, and rural development to create solutions that are practical, affordable, and culturally appropriate.
